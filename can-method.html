How It Works

The CAN Method is not about restriction. It is about alignment. Each category receives a percentage of income so money serves the whole person: responsibilities, healing, future growth, joy, and generosity.

Percentages can change by season. Debt may receive more during a payoff season. Investing may increase once debt is cleared. The structure adapts, but the discipline stays consistent.
